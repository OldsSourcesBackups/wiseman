Contributing to Wiseman

To contribute to the project, please join the project as an Observer, download, print and sign the Contributor Agreement and fax it back to Sun at +1 408-715-2540 or scan and email it to sun_ca@sun.com. Once approved, the agreement will be then published on the Sun Contributor Agreement Signatories List. Contributions can be accepted once the agreement has been approved. After several high-quality contributions, an Observer may be upgraded to Developer and granted write access to the repository.

The wiseman project requires contributors to jointly assign their copyright on contributed code. The Sun Contributor Agreement (SCA) gives Sun and the contributor joint copyright interests in the code: the contributor retains copyrights while also granting those rights to Sun as the open source project sponsor. By executing the SCA, contributors protect the Wiseman code base, enable alternative licensing models, and protect the flexibility to adapt the project to the changing demands of the community. In order for the SCA to be effective, the wiseman project must obtain an assignment for all contributions. Please review the SCA for a complete understanding of its terms and conditions.
